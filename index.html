SDG #4: Quality Education — Universidad Nacional

Problematic What / Why Who Timeline Quiz

Problematic

Misinformation and lack of educational spaces on coexistence and diversity.

The basis of stereotypes and prejudice is ignorance. At the Universidad Nacional this shows as a lack of spaces where people can learn about coexistence, human rights, gender equality and cultural diversity. Our project —based on SDG 4 target 4.7— will create organized talks and this webpage as an educational hub where students can share experiences, get tools to reduce discriminatory situations and access reliable information.

What & Why

What?

We will run talks and build an online resource center to promote respectful, inclusive and supportive spaces for the UNAL community. People will be able to share experiences (rude teachers, staff, classmates) and learn practical tools.

Why?

Discrimination, inequality and environmental problems are reinforced by misinformation and lack of education. If the university does not teach these topics, peace, respect for diversity and care for nature are harder to achieve. University is where we interact most — therefore it is the ideal place to change social dynamics through education.

Who?

We will work mainly with students and educators at Universidad Nacional. They are key agents and are accessible for surveys and interviews.

How & Where

We will do exhaustive research (articles, studies), then design surveys and interviews to adapt solutions to the university context. Implementation will happen on campus — during Bienestar activities, lunch times and other relaxed moments in shared spaces.
