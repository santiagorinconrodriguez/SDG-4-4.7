Survey about UNAL Students’ SDG Knowledge and Perceptions

  1. Oxfam recommends “explore the ‘local in the global’” and “carry out a survey in your local community to identify needs and challenges linked to the SDG themes.” This activity consists of creating and distributing an online survey to understand what UNAL students know about the SDGs and how they perceive them to find direct evidence of awareness gaps and misconceptions to evaluate the actual problematic and the relevance of the project.
